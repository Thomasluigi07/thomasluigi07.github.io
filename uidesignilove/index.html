UI Design I Love


One thing I've been obsessed with for a long time is UI.

For some reason I really like when games/operating systems have intricate looking UIs.

Why? Because I'm CRAAAZY!!!!!

Below is some stuff I think look really cool.


Klonoa: Door to Phantomile & Klonoa 2: Lunatea's Veil (PS1/PS2)

NAMCO

The Klonoa series used to have some cool UI. In the first and second game they had a 3D boss health bar! I don't think I've ever seen that before and oh my god I am always distracted by it every time I play boss levels. However the remakes of those games replaced it with a boring 2D bar.

PIKMIN 1-3 (GameCube/Wii/Wii U/Switch)

Nintendo

Pikmin. Oh my god I love how this series used to have it's UI design. Pikmin 3 is a favorite of mine due to the amount of reflective effects and rotation effects they have on that. 3D UI makes me go "Wow !". However Pikmin 4 throws that uniqueness away with a more flat design. Booooooo!

Xbox 360 Dashboard

Microsoft

The Xbox 360 Dashboard has gone through many revisions. Blades is fast and snappy, NXE introduced the newer Xbox Guide and oh my god I love the sounds in this version. Kinect (Metro v1) had a cool 3D effect when you switched to the kinect mode. Metro (v2) is a little bit boring though.

Wii U Menu & System Software

Nintendo

Honesltly one of my favorite console menus ever. While it takes a while to load due to the amount of stuff going on in it, the amount of cool things hidden in the menu is just awesome. If you rotate the gamepad on the apps screen, the reflection moves. Woah! Even the disc spins for disc based games, with simulated reflection! The buttons floating in the background is cool too. The Wara Wara Plaza is also great too!

Mac OS X (10.4)

Apple

One of the first operating systems I used. The amount of effects they have here is insane, and my god it still looks good. I love when popups appear they roll in from under the title bar. The buttons glowing, askdmklasdj its Just So Good!
